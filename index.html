Open Letter To My Future Self


 Dear future self,

I know right know i'm still lost and wondering if i am really going in a right direction but i'm doing better than the last two years. I'm taking BSIT, i know, unexpected but i'm learning new stuff. It's hard but i'll manage.



We both know i really want a dog. So i hope on the next 5 years we'll be able to get one. Preferably a black one.


And of course, one of the main goal is to graduate. Nothing could make our parents at ease knowing we manage to graduate college.


And i'm guessing 5 years from now i am close in graduating or i already graduate so make sure to find a job.


After i save up enough, i would like to bring my family to Palawan as Papa really wants to go there.


And last but not the least, the main goal is to enjoy the journey that i will have in the next years.

-Your Past Self
